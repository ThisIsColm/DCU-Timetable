DCU Timetable

Sick of having to trudge through the DCU website or Moodle to find your timetable when you're on the move? So were we, so we fixed it.

Developed by a group of CA1 students (so far; you can help on GitHub), DCU Timetable is an Android app for finding your online class timetable when you need it, all from your mobile phone.

With it you can easily grab your online timetable anywhere on the go. It's still in the early stages though, and planned features in the near future include:

  • The ability to store your timetable locally, to save both network usage & battery power.
  • Better display of your timetables, more suited to viewing on your mobile device.
  • Option to track your attendance by using either timed notifications when you can "check in" or automatically through your devices GPS. (wishful thinking)
